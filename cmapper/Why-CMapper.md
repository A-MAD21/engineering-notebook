# Why CMapper Exists

## Introduction

CMapper was created to solve a recurring operational problem: understanding enterprise infrastructure quickly and accurately.

As warehouse environments grew, documentation became outdated, topology knowledge became fragmented, and troubleshooting often started with rebuilding a mental model of the infrastructure instead of solving the incident.

CMapper was designed to reduce that operational uncertainty by continuously discovering infrastructure and building an accurate logical representation of the environment.

## Core Principles

- Discover infrastructure instead of relying on manual documentation.
- Build logical topology from collected data.
- Reduce operational uncertainty.
- Keep the platform modular and extensible.
- Help engineers understand systems before troubleshooting them.

## Vision

CMapper is more than a mapping tool. It is an engineering platform whose goal is to make complex infrastructure easier to understand, operate, and evolve.

Future work (CMapper 2) focuses on modular collectors, continuous discovery, and enterprise-scale infrastructure intelligence.
